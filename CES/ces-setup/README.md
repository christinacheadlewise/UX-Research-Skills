# CES Setup

Monthly kickoff for CES Rolling Research sessions. Runs in two passes:

**Pass 1 (4 days before sessions):**
- Confirms session schedule (default: last Friday of the month, 4 slots at 14:00/14:30/15:00/15:30)
- Creates a monthly Confluence parent page under CES Rolling Research
- Generates a recruitment grid CSV with participant details from the CES survey
- Drafts a Slack question to the squad asking what to explore this month (always sent to Christina's DM for review before posting)

**Pass 2 (day before sessions):**
- Collects Google Calendar viewing links
- Posts session links to Slack
- Updates the "This Week" panel on the Confluence hub page

## Trigger phrases
`ces setup`, `set up ces`, `ces this month`, `ces kickoff`, `monthly ces`, `push ces links`, `ces links`

## Outputs
- `CES/[Month Year]/ces-[month]-[year]-recruit.csv`
- Monthly Confluence page (child of CES Rolling Research)
- Slack posts (draft-first workflow)
