# CES Rolling Research — Discussion Guide
**Session length:** 30 minutes
**Updated:** May 2026
**This month's focus:** Drop-off reasons and signup motivation

---

## Intro (2 min)

**[Moderator: Start recording, confirm consent]**

Thanks for chatting with me today. I'm Christina, a researcher at Wise. This'll take about 30 minutes, no right or wrong answers, and you can skip anything. I'm recording for my notes only.

1. Quick intro — your name, age, where you're based, what you do for work?
2. Who do you bank with and why?

---

## Core Questions: Drop-Off Reasons & Signup Motivation (12 min)

*Objective: Understand why participants started signing up for Wise and what caused them to stop.*

1. What made you decide to sign up for Wise?
   - Where did you hear about it?
   - Did someone recommend it, or did you find it yourself?
   - What were you hoping to use it for?

2. Walk me through what happened when you started signing up.
   - What do you remember about the process?
   - Was anything confusing or unclear?

3. You got to [drop-off point from recruit grid] — what happened there?
   - What were you thinking at that point?
   - Was there something specific that made you stop?
   - Did you plan to come back to it, or were you done?

---

## Stimulus Walkthrough: Signup Screens (14 min)

*Objective: Pinpoint exactly where confusion or friction occurred by showing the actual screens.*

**[Moderator: Share screen / send prototype link. Ask participant to share their screen.]**

4. I'm going to show you the screens from when you signed up. Tell me if anything looks familiar or if something jumps out.

**[Moderator: Walk through screens in order. Pause at each step and ask:]**

5. Do you remember seeing this?
   - What did you think this was asking you to do?
   - Was anything unclear here?

6. **[At their specific drop-off screen:]** This is where you stopped. What's going through your mind looking at this now?
   - Is there anything on this page that put you off?
   - What would have made you keep going?

7. Was there anything you expected to see at this point that wasn't there?

---

## Conclusion (2 min)

1. One thing you'd change about the signup process?

2. Anything else important we didn't cover?

**[Moderator: Thank them, confirm incentive within 5 working days, stop recording]**
