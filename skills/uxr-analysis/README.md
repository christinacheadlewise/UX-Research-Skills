# UXR Analysis

Qualitative analysis thought partner with two modes.

## Mode 1: Coder
Processes transcripts one by one, generates codes (open or framework-based), identifies cross-transcript patterns and emerging themes, and connects findings to past research via Confluence search.

## Mode 2: Devil's Advocate
Takes Christina's interpretations, themes, or hypotheses and stress-tests them using 8 challenge lenses: alternative explanations, sample bias, confirmation bias, "so what" test, strength of evidence, missing voices, stakeholder lens, and temporal validity.

## How it works
1. Asks which mode
2. **Coder:** processes transcripts sequentially, presents codes per transcript, then surfaces cross-transcript themes with frequency counts
3. **Challenge:** listens to Christina's thinking, then deploys 2-3 relevant challenges one at a time, iterating until the analysis is sharp

## Trigger phrases
`uxr analysis`, `analyse this`, `code this`, `run analysis`, `what are the themes`, `challenge me`, `devil's advocate`, `push back on this`

## Outputs
- Coded themes with evidence (markdown file, Confluence appendix, or held in conversation)
- Solid ground / needs more evidence summary (Challenge mode)
