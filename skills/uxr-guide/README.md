# UXR Guide

Generates a timed, structured discussion guide and publishes it to Confluence as a child page under the relevant research plan.

## How it works
1. Pulls research goal, questions, methodology, and session length from an existing research plan (or asks directly)
2. Asks about topics, activities, and stimulus materials one question at a time
3. Builds a guide with timed sections, objectives, numbered questions, probes, and moderator notes
4. Presents for review, iterates until approved
5. Publishes to Confluence under the research plan page

## Trigger phrases
`uxr guide`, `write a discussion guide`, `new discussion guide`, `create a guide`, `build a moderator guide`

## Outputs
- Confluence page: "Discussion Guide: [Study Name]"
